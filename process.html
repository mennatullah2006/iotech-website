Our 4-Step Execution Process

1

Site Inspection & Measurements

We measure the site area, identify structural elements, provide suggestions for space redistribution, create hypothetical furniture layouts, prepare estimated quotes, and determine required timelines.

2

Interior Design & 3D Visualization

We redistribute walls and spaces, provide material selection suggestions, distribute furniture in a semi-final manner, determine electrical appliances types, identify kitchen and bathroom needs, create preliminary 3D models, and approve designs before execution.

3

Engineering Plans

We prepare detailed engineering plans with real dimensions according to furniture layouts. This includes electrical plans, load distribution, supply and drainage plans, floor plans, ceiling plans, decoration plans, bathroom plans, and smart home finishing plans.

4

Material Selection & Purchasing

We help you choose materials and home requirements easily and quickly. We purchase devices and sets, and obtain discounts for customers from the best suppliers. Don't worry when you're with us at iotech Cairo finishing company.
